Favorite Travel Gear

  • Backpack or Carry-On: Lightweight, durable, and fits airline requirements—like the Nomatic or Osprey Farpoint.
  • Travel Adapter: Universal adapters with USB ports are a must for international trips.
  • Reusable Water Bottle: Eco-friendly and essential for staying hydrated—bonus if it’s collapsible.
  • Lightweight Rain Jacket: Compact and reliable for unpredictable weather.
